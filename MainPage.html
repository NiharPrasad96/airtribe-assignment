HPCYOA!

The Harry Potter Create Your Own Adventure!

The Harry Potter Create Your Own Adventure (HPCYOA) was built as a fun diversion, to explore the world of Harry Potter in an interactive and exciting way. In the HPCYOA, you are in control of your own unique witch or wizard. Many decisions will confront you along your journey, and you will have to choose wisely or face the dire, often deadly, consequences. Are you ready to get started?
  • In The Dark Forest
    • Creatures and Artifacts!

    Tag along as a fellow Hogwards student where we explore the dark forest .Your choices decide whether you will encounter a beast or a treasure.



  • Escape from Azkaban
    • Escape with Serius Black!

    You and your Cell mate Serius Black finally escaped the dementors but are you safe out here? Tag along to find out..
